Resources

This page will be updated with various drone related resources

YouTubers

  • Joshua Bardwell | A skilled FPV pilot that does extremely detailed videos about the software and hardware related to drones.
  • UAVfutures | A more laid back channel with fun content and various build guides.
  • Rotor Riot | A group of talented FPV pilots that post videos weekly. They're known for their crazy stunts and cool videos.

Websites

  • Oscar Liang | In-depth tutorials about all things FPV.

Stores

  • Banggood | This is where Aaron and Wes started their FPV careers. Banggood has an enormous selection of FPV and other tech stuff. Their prices are relatively cheap but be warned that they ship from Asia, so things could take from weeks to months to arrive.
  • Gearbest | Another online store similar to Banggood. They don't have as much selection as Banggood but sometimes you can find better deals here.
  • SN Hobbies | A local retailer located in New West. They have tons of FPV parts and equipment, though much of it is on the higher expensive end.
